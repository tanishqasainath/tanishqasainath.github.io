About Me

I believe in the beauty of intentional living — where every detail, every moment, and every connection carries meaning.

With a passion for design, technology, and human connection, I bring curiosity and warmth to everything I do. Whether it's crafting visual experiences, solving complex problems, or simply sharing a good cup of coffee with someone I care about.

Originally from India, now exploring the world one city at a time. I find joy in the small things — golden hour light, the smell of rain on dry earth, handwritten letters, and conversations that go past midnight.
