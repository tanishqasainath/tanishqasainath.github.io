About Me

A BA LLB graduate with a passion for corporate law, compliance, and making the complex feel simple.

I've worked across corporate compliance, data privacy (DPDP Act), contract management, and IP due diligence—helping organisations stay on the right side of the law while moving fast. At Witmer.AI and USDC Global, I built compliance frameworks that scaled.

Based in Bengaluru, I bring a multi-disciplinary perspective shaped by internships at Fox Mandal & Associates and the Global Fintech Summit. I speak English, Hindi, Kannada, Marathi, and basic Tamil.

Outside the office, you'll find me volunteering with Sevayana Trust, writing poetry, at the cinema, or behind a keyboard — I play percussion too.
